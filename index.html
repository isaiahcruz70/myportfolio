Who is Isaiah Cruz?

I was raised by a single mom even with her struggles she pushed me to be the best person I can be. My main goal in life was to become a police officer but didn't work out for me mentally. My eyes were opened by my pastor who did coding as a career. He then introduced me to the beautiful staff of Bitwise. Next thing you know I'm taking classes at Geekwise. My main goal in life is to become a future employee of Bitwise so I can give other kids the opportunity to change their lives around.
